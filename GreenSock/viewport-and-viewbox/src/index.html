Viewport is the size of the viewable area.

ViewBox controls what shows up in the viewable area. Like zoom or panning.

The first 2 numbers in our viewBox are the starting coordinates of the viewBox: viewBox="0 0 500 500"

The second 2 numbers in our viewBox are the width and height. Making the numbers larger or smaller will zoom the SVG in or out. viewBox="0 0 500 500"
